about us

Namaste ! We, the management team and staffs of Hotel Kanjirowa jumla welcome you whole-heartedly and we shall do everything possible to make your stay pleasant, interesting and a wonderful experience with traditional Tibetan and Nepalese culture.

Hotel Kanjirowa is run by a two brothers, Norbu Jumli and Jambal lama from a Mugali family, with warm Tibetan Hospitality and culture.

Hotel Kanjirowa lies in the heart of Jumla Valley and is in very close proximity with Jumla airport. Here, at our hotel , you will be greeted at our door with charming smiles, and thereafter you will be engulfed in warm Tibetan Hospitality.

Within jumla itself, you can find interesting places to visit. The jumla valley has almost everything for rest and recreation, from the majestic scenic beauty to more energetic pursuits like trekking and river rafting. Jumla has some of the best known mountains virtually on its doorstep â€“ the Kanjirowa Patarasi mountain, Sisne Hiuchuli Himalaya. Hotel kanjirowa is well-known among trekkers too. The valley, lakes and mountains are the distillation of Nepal's scenic beauty, which makes Jumla a photographers' dreamspace too. The climate is good for most of the year and the flora and fauna are beautiful too. The valley is also a shopper's paradise with a wide variety of artifacts and other essential needs. There is a gentle village atmosphere in jumla and Jumli people are wonderfully hospitable.

Let us an opportunity to help you explore the paradise.
